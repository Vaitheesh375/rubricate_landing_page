Refund Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Rubricate provides expert services and project-based delivery workflows. Because services are custom and resource-backed, refund eligibility depends on project stage and fulfillment status.

Before Work Starts

If a project is canceled before expert allocation or deliverable preparation begins, payments are generally refundable, minus any payment processing fees where applicable.

After Work Starts

Once work has started, partial refunds may be provided only for undelivered scope. Completed milestones, accepted outputs, and verified expert hours are non-refundable.

Disputes

If you believe a deliverable does not meet agreed acceptance criteria, contact support within 7 days of delivery. We will review project records and propose remediation or a fair adjustment.

Contact

For refund requests, contact support with your project ID and invoice details so we can process your request quickly.
