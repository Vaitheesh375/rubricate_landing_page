Quality System

How we ensure
exceptional output

🎯
Credential-Based Expert Vetting

Every expert on Rubricate is verified — degrees, professional licenses, and domain knowledge assessments are validated before any project access is granted.

⚖️
Inter-Rater Reliability Scoring

Multiple qualified experts review the same samples. Statistical agreement measures flag inconsistencies and surface disagreements for human audit before delivery.

🔁
Calibration Tasks

Golden-standard examples with known correct answers are embedded in every project batch. Expert performance against these calibration items is monitored continuously.

📋
Structured Guidelines

Every project ships with detailed task guidelines, worked examples, and edge case documentation developed collaboratively with the client before work begins.

👁️
Real-Time Review Queue

A senior quality reviewer monitors output in real-time for complex or sensitive projects. Issues are caught before they propagate through your dataset.

🛡️
Full Audit Trail

Every decision is logged: who reviewed it, when, what the response was, and what quality score it received. Full transparency for compliance and model documentation.

What types of tasks can Rubricate handle? +

Rubricate supports a wide range of AI-adjacent expert tasks: annotation and labeling, preference ranking and RLHF data generation, model output evaluation, red-teaming and safety testing, human-in-the-loop review of live AI outputs, domain-specific data generation, translation and localization quality, and more. If your task requires genuine expert judgment, we can scope it.

How do you ensure expert quality? +

Every expert is credential-verified and passes a domain-specific assessment before onboarding. During active projects, we use calibration tasks, inter-rater agreement monitoring, and human review queues. Quality problems are caught and corrected before they reach you.

How quickly can a project get started? +

For standard domains with existing expert pools, we can move from signed proposal to active experts in under 48 hours. Niche domains requiring custom sourcing typically take 1–2 additional weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during the scoping call.

Is my data kept confidential? +

Absolutely. All experts sign NDAs before accessing any client project. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified, and we support HIPAA and GDPR compliance requirements. Data access is role-based and logged. Your proprietary materials never leave our secure environment without your explicit permission.

Can I integrate Rubricate with my existing ML pipeline? +

Yes. We offer a RESTful API and webhook support for triggering review tasks from your own systems. Common integrations include automatically routing low-confidence model outputs to human review, batch uploading datasets for annotation, and receiving results back in JSONL, CSV, or custom formats.

Do I need AI experience to become an expert? +

No. We provide full onboarding and task-specific training for every project. What we need from you is deep domain knowledge — whether that's clinical medicine, contract law, or Python engineering. The AI context is something we teach; the expertise is something only you can bring.
